### 2. Jason Lemkin's Day Running 25+ Live AI Agents — and the Mistake That Almost Broke One
- **Hook:** Jason Lemkin, founder of SaaStr, posted his actual daily schedule managing 25+ live AI agents — and then followed it up with a confession: they stopped monitoring one of their agents and it quietly degraded. Real talk from someone running AI at scale.
- **Why it matters:** Lemkin's day looks like this: 60 minutes reviewing agent outputs at dawn, vibe-coding new projects mid-morning, then back to updating agents in production in the afternoon. His warning — "We stopped checking in with a few of them every day. The less monitored ones got worse." — is the kind of operational lesson you can't get from a tutorial.
- **Implication:** This is the unglamorous reality of running AI agents in production that nobody talks about enough. Building the agent is 20% of the work. Monitoring, retraining, and maintaining it is the other 80%. If you're deploying agents for yourself or clients, build your review loop *before* you deploy, not after something breaks.
  - **Builders:** Before you ship an agent to a client, build a daily review ritual into the contract — a 15-minute check-in on outputs, drift, and errors. Clients won't ask for it. But when an unmonitored agent degrades quietly and costs them a deal, they'll blame you. Make the review loop part of your service, not an afterthought.
  - **Marketers:** If you're running AI agents for content, social, or email — assign one person the job of reading the outputs every morning. Not to approve everything, but to catch when tone shifts, brand voice drifts, or the agent starts repeating itself. Lemkin's lesson applies here exactly: the unmonitored ones get worse.
  - **Recruiters:** If you've deployed an AI screening agent or an outreach sequence, check whether it's still calibrated to what you actually want. Scoring models drift as the job market shifts. An agent trained on your ideal candidate profile from 6 months ago may now be filtering out people you'd hire today.
- **Type:** Thought Leader
- **URL:** [Jason Lemkin on X — daily agent workflow](https://x.com/jasonlk/status/2035747161183580489) / [Mistake post](https://x.com/jasonlk/status/2035792251679633708)
